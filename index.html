Robotics Team Overview

Our Robotics Team is a dynamic group of young innovators passionate about engineering and robotics. We design, build, and program robots to conquer real-world challenges, combining creativity and technical prowess to inspire future engineers and technology enthusiasts.

Robotic System Architect

As Robotic System Architects, we are the architects of our mechanical and electronic wonders. We craft, maintain, and fine-tune the core systems that empower our robots to excel in the world of automation and innovation.

First Tech Challenge

First Tech Challenge (FTC) is an exhilarating robotics competition where young innovators design, build, and program robots to take on real-world engineering challenges. It's a platform where creativity and technical skills unite to inspire the engineers of tomorrow.
